## Practical Implications

| Complexity | n=10 | n=100 | n=1000 |
|-----------|------|-------|--------|
| O(1) | 1 | 1 | 1 |
| O(log n) | 3 | 7 | 10 |
| O(n) | 10 | 100 | 1000 |
| O(n log n) | 33 | 664 | 9966 |
| O(n²) | 100 | 10,000 | 1,000,000 |

## Choosing the Right Algorithm

- **Small datasets (n < 50)**: Simple O(n²) algorithms often win due to low overhead
- **Medium datasets**: O(n log n) algorithms like merge sort or quick sort
- **Large datasets**: O(n log n) or better, consider space trade-offs
