## Paired Delimiters

Use `\left` and `\right` to match delimiter height to content:

$$
\left\{ \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{1}{n^2} \right\}
$$

```latex
\left\{ \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{1}{n^2} \right\}
```

For invisible delimiters, use `\left.` or `\right.`:

$$
\left. \frac{dy}{dx} \right|_{x=0}
$$

```latex
\left. \frac{dy}{dx} \right|_{x=0}
```
