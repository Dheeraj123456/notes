# Statistics

**Statistics** is the science of collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data.

## Topics

- [[ds/statistics/probability|Probability Theory]] — foundations of uncertainty

## Key Concepts

- **Descriptive statistics**: summarizing data (mean, median, variance)
- **Inferential statistics**: drawing conclusions from samples
- **Hypothesis testing**: determining statistical significance
